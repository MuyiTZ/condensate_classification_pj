# Executive Summary

This control-guided analysis compares target-protein track dynamics against NLS, H2B, and MCM2_Bound controls. The results identify candidate target-specific trapping-like dynamics, not definitive condensate localization.

NLS was used as the free-like nuclear mobility control, H2B as the chromatin-bound confinement control, and MCM2_Bound as the replication/chromatin-associated bound-motion control.
